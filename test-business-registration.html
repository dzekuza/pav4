🔧 Business Registration Test

✅ Multi-Step Business Registration Verification

Step 1: Access Business Login

Navigate to the business login page:

http://localhost:8083/business-login

Step 2: Click Register Button

On the business login page, click the "Don't have an account? Register" button

Expected Result: Step 1 - Account Setup

You should see a form with:

  • ✅ Email Address field
  • ✅ Password field
  • ✅ Confirm Password field
  • ✅ Progress indicator showing "Step 1 of 2"
  • ✅ "Next Step" button

Expected Result: Step 2 - Business Information

After clicking "Next Step", you should see:

  • ✅ Business Name field
  • ✅ Domain field
  • ✅ Website URL field
  • ✅ Business Description field
  • ✅ Category dropdown
  • ✅ Country dropdown
  • ✅ Contact Email field
  • ✅ Contact Phone field
  • ✅ Business Address field
  • ✅ "Previous Step" and "Create Business Account" buttons

🔍 Troubleshooting

If you don't see the email/password fields:

  1. Make sure you clicked "Don't have an account? Register"
  2. Check the browser console for any JavaScript errors
  3. Verify the server is running on port 8081
  4. Try refreshing the page

If the registration wizard doesn't appear:

  1. Check that the BusinessRegistrationWizard component is properly imported
  2. Verify the showRegistrationWizard state is being set to true
  3. Check for any React component errors in the console
